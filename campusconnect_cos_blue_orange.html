About CampusConnect

CampusConnect is a free mobile app built by students for students at College of the Sequoias. We created it to solve a real problem: there's no single place to find out what's happening on campus.

Our mission

We're building tools that connect COS students to their campus community. By centralizing events, clubs, and campus activities, we help everyone discover communities that matter to them.

What's coming next

  • User accounts to save your clubs and events across devices
  • Follow clubs to get updates from communities you care about
  • Direct messaging with club organizers
  • QR code check-in for events
  • Integration with COS calendar and facilities

Built by the CS Club

CampusConnect is built and maintained by students in the College of the Sequoias Computer Science Club as a portfolio project. We're learning modern mobile development with React Native, Expo, and Supabase.
